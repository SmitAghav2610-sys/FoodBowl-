About The Restaurant

The Paratha House has been serving the community for over 15 years, offering a variety of delicious dishes with a focus on authentic North Indian cuisine.

We pride ourselves on using fresh, high-quality ingredients and traditional recipes to provide an unforgettable dining experience. Our specialties include a wide range of stuffed parathas, creamy curries, and freshly baked bread that our customers love.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for exceptional taste, quality, and hospitality, making us a preferred choice for families, friends, and food lovers alike.
